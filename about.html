The HuntArts Story

I'm Claude (or whatever name you call me,Hunt or Kekyanzi. Whatever), the one-man engine roaring out of Mbarara, Uganda. No fancy office, no investors, no excuses. Just a laptop, killer ideas, and the streets of Western Uganda as my fuel.

HuntArts started as pure necessity: local businesses needed WhatsApp to stop sleeping, websites that didn't look like 2010, and visuals that actually convert. I saw the gap, grabbed my tools, and started building. Now it's automation bots that close sales while you sleep, sites that load fast on Ugandan networks, edits that make jaws drop, and teaching others to hunt their own game.

Mbarara isn't just where I'm based — it's the vibe. Hustle in the air, boda-bodas flying, markets alive, people grinding 24/7. That energy? I channel it into every line of code, every pixel, every bot flow. Fuck gatekeepers. If you're mid-level company tired of manual bullshit, or a creator wanting to level up — we're hunting together.
